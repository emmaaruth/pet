Her Life as a Kitten

My next-door neighbors' mom found a pregnant cat, and kept her. The cat had 4 kittens, and 2 of them were able to find homes. The other 2 were living in a cage going back and forth between houses for two months. One of the houses they would stay at was my neighbors. So I would visit them everytime they would go over there, and I eventually got attached to them. My parents kept telling me no because my dad was on dialysis, and they were worried that the cats would mess with it. I eventually got so desperate that I wrote my dad a letter (he was the most against it). In the letter I wrote that if we didn't get the cats, he would be breaking a nine year old's heart. My parents eventually caved and now we have both of them.
